## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Scalability**: Architect for scale from day 1
- **Security**: Regular audits, penetration testing
- **Performance**: Continuous monitoring and optimization

### Business Risks
- **Competition**: Focus on unique value (safety, activities)
- **User acquisition**: Strong referral program
- **Retention**: Engagement features, community building

### Market Risks
- **Regulatory**: Stay informed on privacy laws
- **Safety incidents**: Robust reporting and moderation
- **Market fit**: Continuous user feedback and iteration
